
# **Reconnaissance Signal Detector — Full Implementation Blueprint**

This subsystem detects:

- Cyber reconnaissance (scanning, enumeration, probing)  
- Physical reconnaissance (surveillance, casing, pattern‑mapping)  
- HUMINT reconnaissance (targeting, elicitation, social engineering)  
- Influence reconnaissance (audience testing, narrative probing)  
- Supply‑chain reconnaissance (vendor mapping, dependency probing)  

It fuses **multi‑vector telemetry**, **behavioral signals**, and **threat‑actor TTPs** to identify early‑stage hostile intent.

---

## **1. System Architecture Overview**

### Core Components
- **Recon Telemetry Collector** — ingests cyber, physical, HUMINT, and OSINT reconnaissance signals  
- **Behavioral Baseline Engine** — establishes normal patterns for identities, facilities, and networks  
- **Recon Feature Engine** — extracts reconnaissance‑specific features  
- **Anomaly Detection Engine** — identifies deviations from baseline  
- **Threat Scenario Engine** — models known recon TTPs  
- **Correlation Layer** — links recon signals to other threat vectors  
- **Fusion Output Layer** — pushes alerts to dashboards & briefings  

---

### A. Cyber Recon Signals
- Port scanning

### B. Physical Recon Signals
- Repeated perimeter visits

- Tailgating attempts

### C. HUMINT Recon Signals
- Social engineering attempts  
- Elicitation behaviors  
- Foreign contact attempts  
- Unusual meeting patterns  
- Insider targeting  

### D. Influence Recon Signals
- Narrative testing  
- Audience probing  
- Micro‑targeted messaging  
- Botnet “test bursts”  

### E. Supply Chain Recon Signals
- Vendor mapping  
- Dependency probing  
- Third‑party enumeration  
- Procurement intelligence gathering  

---

## **3. Recon Feature Engineering**

### Core Feature Categories
- **Surface Mapping Indicators** — scanning, enumeration, probing  
- **Pattern Repetition** — repeated visits, repeated queries  
- **Target Selection Behavior** — narrowing focus on specific assets or people  
- **Temporal Drift** — changes in timing or frequency  
- **Cross‑Vector Recon Alignment** — cyber + physical + HUMINT alignment  
- **Actor Capability Indicators** — sophistication, tooling, infrastructure  

### Feature Storage
- Stored in time‑series DB  
- Indexed by identity, asset, facility, and IP/host  
- Supports sliding‑window analysis (1h, 24h, 7d, 30d)

## **4. Anomaly Detection Engine**

### ML Models
- Isolation Forest for recon anomalies  
- LSTM models for reconnaissance drift  
- GNNs for infrastructure mapping  
- Bayesian changepoint detection for sudden recon spikes  

### Rule‑Based Models
- Known recon TTPs (MITRE PRE‑ATT&CK)  
- Multi‑vector recon patterns  
- High‑risk asset targeting  
- Recon → intrusion escalation patterns  

### Output
- Severity score  
- Confidence score  
- Contributing features  
- Recommended actions

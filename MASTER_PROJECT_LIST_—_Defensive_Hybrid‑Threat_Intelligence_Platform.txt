# 📘 **DESCRIPTIONS OF EACH SYSTEM COMPONENT**
Below is the high‑level description of each subsystem so you know exactly what you’re building.

---

## **1. Zero‑Trust Identity Layer**
A hardened identity system enforcing least privilege, continuous verification, and real‑time anomaly detection.

---

## **2. Threat Hunting Engine**
A rule‑driven and ML‑driven system that continuously scans for APT behaviors across logs, endpoints, and network flows.

---

## **3. Access Zoning & Badge Intelligence**
Physical access logs fused with behavioral analytics to detect suspicious movement patterns.

---

## **4. Narrative Monitoring Platform**
Tracks cross‑platform information flows to detect coordinated disinformation or destabilization narratives.

---

## **5. Cross‑Vector Correlation Engine**
The heart of the fusion center: correlates cyber, physical, HUMINT, and influence signals into unified threat events.

---

## **6. Diplomatic Activity Monitor**
Detects anomalous patterns in foreign diplomatic presence, travel, or contact attempts.

---

## **7. Hybrid Threat IR Platform**
A unified incident response system that handles cyber, physical, and influence incidents under one workflow.

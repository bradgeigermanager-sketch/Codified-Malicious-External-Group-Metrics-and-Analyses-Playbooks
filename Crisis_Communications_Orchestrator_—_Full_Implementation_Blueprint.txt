
# **Crisis Communications Orchestrator — Full Implementation Blueprint**

This subsystem coordinates **internal and external communications** during cyber, physical, HUMINT, influence, or geopolitical crises.

It ensures:

- Clear, authoritative messaging  
- Rapid dissemination  
- Misinformation suppression  
- Leadership alignment  
- Legal/compliance adherence  
- Multi‑channel delivery  
- Zero‑ambiguity decision support  

It is the **communications backbone** of the crisis‑response architecture.

---

## **1. System Architecture Overview**

### Core Components
- **Crisis Message Engine**  
- **Audience Segmentation & Routing Layer**  
- **Approval & Governance Workflow**  
- **Multi‑Channel Delivery Engine**  
- **Misinformation Counter‑Messaging Module**  
- **Crisis Timeline & Status Board**  
- **Integration Layer (IR, Leadership, Dashboard)**  

---

## **2. Crisis Message Engine**

This is where crisis messages are generated, templated, validated, and approved.

### Capabilities
- Pre‑built crisis templates  
- AI‑assisted message drafting  
- Severity‑based messaging tiers  
- Multi‑vector message variants  
- Auto‑summaries for leadership  
- Legal/compliance review hooks  

### Message Types
- Internal alerts  
- Executive updates  
- Public statements  
- Regulatory notifications  
- Vendor/partner communications  
- Media holding statements

- Severity‑based

## **4. Approval & Governance Workflow**

### Workflow Features
- Multi‑party approval  
- Legal/compliance review  
- Leadership override capability  
- Crisis‑mode accelerated approvals  
- Full audit logging  

### Approval Chains
- Cyber incident → CISO + Legal  
- Physical incident → CSO + Legal  
- Influence‑ops → Comms Director + Intel Lead  
- Diplomatic/geopolitical → CEO + Government Affairs

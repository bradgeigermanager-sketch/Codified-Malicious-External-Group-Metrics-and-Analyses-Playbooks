## Domain 1: Digital & Cyber Operations (DCO)
Focuses on the technical, infrastructure, and backend execution layers used to establish a baseline presence in the target nation's digital ecosystem.
## Sub-Domain 1.1: Network Evasion & Automation

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC A: Algorithmic Mimicry
* Focus: Evading automated security filters, managing bot lifespans, optimizing hosting/token costs, and mimicking authentic human digital behavior.

## Sub-Domain 1.2: Synthetic Platform Architecture

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC C: Adversarial AI Cybersquatting
* Focus: The automated creation of lookalike technical infrastructure, domain hijacking, layout scraping, and generating scalable, AI-driven fake distribution nodes.

------------------------------
## Domain 2: Cognitive & Information Warfare (CIW)
Focuses on psychological manipulation, narrative weaponization, and the long-term exploitation of societal and political vulnerabilities.
## Sub-Domain 2.1: Hyper-Targeted Audience Engagement

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC B: Data-Driven Narrative Wedge
* Focus: Scraping domestic marketing data, isolating hyper-local geographic or municipal grievances, and forcing high organic amplification by real users.

## Sub-Domain 2.2: Strategic Societal Disruption

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC D: Continuous Outrage Inoculation
* Focus: Overloading public consciousness, driving political polarization indices, and manufacturing artificial micro-crises to paralyze domestic policymaking.

------------------------------
## Domain 3: Physical HUMINT & Infiltration (PHI)
Focuses on the deployment of physical human assets on the ground, bypassing border controls, and exploiting international legal protocols.
## Sub-Domain 3.1: Legal & Sovereign Mechanism Exploitation

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC E: Diplomatic Cover Operations
   * TACTIC F: State-Issued Identity Laundering
* Focus: Weaponizing international treaties (like the Vienna Convention) and utilizing authentic, cryptographically valid government documents issued under fabricated names to pass border biometrics.

## Sub-Domain 3.2: Deep-Cover Field Operations

* Tactics Included:
* TACTIC G: Deep-Cover "Illegals"
   * TACTIC H: Digital-Physical Convergence
* Focus: Multi-transit travel obfuscation, acquisition of neutral third-party documentation, multi-year community seeding, and ground-level financing or handling of domestic assets.
